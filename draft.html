Hill Farm Inn

Hill Farm is an inn, restaurant and farm just outside of Manchester, VT. We invite you to enjoy the many activities on the property and encourage you to take advantage of all that Manchester and southern Vermont have to offer.

Hill Farm es un hotel, restaurante y granja en las afueras de Manchester, Vermont. Los invitamos a disfrutar de las actividades, la naturaleza y todo lo demás que ofrece el sur de Vermont en el otoño.

We have reserved the entire Inn for our guests. All rooms include breakfast and complete access to the property.

Q & A

Preguntas

Can I bring a plus one?

Your plus one will have already been invited.

Should I bring my children?

We would love if you brought your children, but no pressure! We will have multiple babysitters during the wedding receptions so you can relax and have fun. The Inn and farm are very kid friendly, as is the entire Manchester area. However, if you’d like a weekend away, please use our wedding to take one. Please remember to include your kid(s) in your RSVP.

What should I wear?

Código de vestimenta

We encourage everyone to wear what makes them comfortable. That includes sneakers! Friday and Sunday are a great time to wear your jeans or something similar. For Saturday, you should still focus on comfort. There is no need for a suit or cocktail dress unless that is what you want to wear (if there is a dress you need an excuse to wear, then we strongly encourage it!). For women, please dress so you are comfortable with the weather. Please remember that these events will be outside in a field, so pointy heels will not be practical.

Queremos que esten cómodos. ¡Eso incluye los tenis! El viernes y el domingo son ideales para usar lo que usarian para un almuerzo campestre. Para el sábado, la prioridad debe seguir siendo la comodidad. No es necesario usar traje ni vestido de coctel. Los eventos seran al aire libre, no recomendamos usar tacones.
